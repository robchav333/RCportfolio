Robert Chavez Teapot

Artist Statement

TThe teapot exercise helped me develop new skills and use new tool sets in Fusion 360. I started off with tutorial video that instructor in Art 103 had us follow. We used the revolve, loft, and sweep tools. After completion of this exercise, I added some primary hue colors to add a fun look to it. I then created a customized teapot that derived from my drawing sketch below. I intended to make the teapot abstract and futuristic. Using the same tools before like revolve and sweep, I finalized the piece with rounded curves for the body. Also, for the handle and pour spout I made them have a curved formation. Finally added a see-through glass texture for a creative look.
